 About Me


Welcome to UnderdogTrading.net, my transparent trading journey.I’m a retail trader learning to navigate the markets the hard way. This project is not about flashy wins or “get rich quick” promises. It’s about building a sustainable trading career through proper risk management, strict trading rules, and consistent execution.

In the past, my biggest losses didn’t come from bad setups; they came from violating my own rules. I would add to losing positions, hold trades far longer than planned, and let emotions take control. Those painful experiences taught me the most valuable lesson in trading: the system only works if you actually follow it.

Right now, I’m in the process of fully trusting my trading system. I’ve started with micro bets and will slowly scale up over time. This deliberate approach is helping me rewire my brain, to focus on flawless execution rather than outcomes, and to trade without being hijacked by greed or fear.


Why I'm Sharing Everything Publicly

I trade noticeably better when I know people are watching. Posting my trades and journal creates real accountability, it's like having a trading partner looking over my shoulder. I also want to connect with other traders who are on the same journey. Whether you’re a beginner or experienced, I welcome your thoughts, ideas, and feedback. Together we can learn and improve faster.

This is a real, unfiltered journey, my wins, losses, lessons, and growth. No hype. Just honest progress.Thank you for following along.
Let’s grow together!
